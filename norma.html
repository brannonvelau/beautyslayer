Momma G

Public Relations/Retail Manager

My name is Norma Grzesiowski and I am the proud Mother of two amazing daughters - Heather Lombardo (Premier Golf Cars) and Angela Lynn "Hairslayer." I have lived in Lake Havasu for over 53 years. I am retired, married to an amazing man Larry G. Our daughters and grandchildren all were born here in LHC so we have three generations of graduates from Lake Havasu High School as well as working together here at Beauty Slayer's Salon & Suites. I love working in the Beauty Slayer's Salon as Angela's Assistant and Public Relations person. I think that is a rather "stuffy" title but the one that I like the most is the name lovingly given to me from the Slayer Family as "Momma G." I do whatever I can to be helpful and supportive of all the amazing talented gals & guy in our salon. Every person who walks in our Salon starts out as a client and walks out as a part of our Slayer Family. We enjoy serving our community and being involved in many aspects of assisting Non-Profits with supplies, donations etc. I look forward to meeting you when you come to visit us as we welcome you to our beautifully decorated salon.
